Boxing Apocalypse

by Gotham City Films

for PS4, Steam, Oculus, Vive, and Arcade Machines

Boxing Apocalypse, a Sci-Fi VR boxing game, lets you punch your way through a horde of aliens, and even other players in online multiplayer fights. Realistic controls with some unique weapons lead to an exuberant experience matched by no other game. Eric’s main contributions to this project include mixed reality, and publishing on Playstation 4.

Hack, Slash & Backstab

by MAGIC Spell Studios

for Xbox One, Steam (Humble Bundle), and Arcade Machines

Hack, Slash & Backstab is a local co-op hack-and-slash dungeon crawler. The objective being to fight through and explore a randomized dungeon that is full of treasure and enemies no person can tackle alone. Friends become enemies as friendly fire is always on and only the first person through the portal can escape the dungeon as the winner. Eric’s main contributions to this project include R&D, heavy graphics coding and optimizations, as well as the publishing on Steam and Xbox One.

Space Exodus

by Manna Entertainment

for Android

Space Exodus is an android game is reminiscent of the classic arcade space shooter. Fighting alone against wave after wave of enemy ship, with different modes ranging from a time attack to escort missions, and choreographed boss battles mixed in. Eric’s main contributions to this project were the logic behind the enemies and publishing to android.

Dont Tap the Glass

by yparc

for Windows 8 Store

This is a simple game where you tap around on the glass of an aquarium and attempt to scare off the fish before time runs out. While the gameplay is entertaining, the general design falls short. However the greatest aspect of this game is that it spawned off multiple highly successful games of similar name and gameplay on other touch screen platforms.
